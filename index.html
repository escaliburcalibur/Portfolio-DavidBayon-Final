Project Information


Virgil Abloh reshaped contemporary culture by dissolving the boundaries between art, fashion, music, and graphic design. As one of the most influential designers of our time, his work extended far beyond products, becoming a language and a methodology that redefined how creativity could exist within and outside institutions.


LEGACY is an editorial fanzine dedicated to honoring Abloh’s impact and preserving his vision. Conceived as a tribute, the project seeks to highlight his most important works while reflecting the multidisciplinary spirit that defined his practice. Rather than functioning as a retrospective, LEGACY positions itself as a cultural artifact—one that mirrors the way Abloh communicated ideas through design.


The objective of the project was to create a visually striking narrative that blends art, fashion, and graphic design, echoing the way Abloh himself operated across disciplines. The fanzine format was intentionally chosen for its raw, independent nature, aligning with Abloh’s constant dialogue between high culture and street-level experimentation.


The guiding concept behind LEGACY is celebration through interpretation. Instead of simply documenting his work, the project translates Abloh’s design philosophy into layout, typography, and composition—embracing contrast, fragmentation, and bold visual hierarchy to reflect his approach to questioning systems and remixing references.


Visually, the editorial language draws from graphic design as both content and medium. Layouts prioritize strong rhythm and tension, allowing imagery and text to coexist dynamically. The composition acts as a narrative device, guiding the reader through Abloh’s influence while reinforcing the idea of design as a cultural conversation rather than a static outcome.


At its core, LEGACY is a homage not only to Virgil Abloh’s body of work, but to his mindset: an invitation to think differently, cross disciplines fearlessly, and view design as a tool for cultural change. Through this project, the fanzine becomes a space where his influence continues to live, inspire, and provoke new interpretations within contemporary culture.

Project Information


In a category often defined by excess and ornamentation, Vino Claro emerges as a wine brand rooted in purity, simplicity, and honesty. The brand concept revolves around clarity—not only as a visual attribute, but as a value that speaks to transparency in process, flavor, and identity.


This packaging design project was developed to translate that philosophy into a tangible, sensory experience. The challenge was to create a system that visually communicates clarity while standing apart from traditional wine packaging codes. Rather than relying on decorative elements, the design embraces reduction and material expression as its core language.


The objective was to explore how transparency could function as both a conceptual and physical element within packaging design. By playing with translucent materials and clean graphic interventions, the packaging allows the product itself to become part of the visual narrative, reinforcing the idea that nothing is hidden.


The solution manifests through a restrained yet intentional design system where clarity guides every decision. Typography, layout, and material choices work together to create a sense of lightness and openness, allowing the wine to speak for itself. The packaging becomes an extension of the brand’s promise: purity without artifice.


A dedicated version for white wine, Vino Blanco Claro, further emphasizes this concept. Lighter tones and enhanced transparency amplify the perception of freshness and refinement, while maintaining consistency within the overall brand system. The result is a cohesive yet flexible identity that adapts naturally across variations without losing its essence.


Ultimately, the project positions Vino Claro as a brand that values authenticity over embellishment. Through clarity in form, material, and message, the packaging reframes wine not as a luxury object defined by excess, but as a product defined by honesty and balance.
